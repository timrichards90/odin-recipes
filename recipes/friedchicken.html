Fried Chicken

Southern Style

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups flour
  • 3/4 cup corn flour
  • 3 tsp salt
  • 1.5 tbsp black pepper
  • 1.5 tsp sweet paprika
  • 1/2 tsp cayenna peepper
    • >
  • 3 tsp garlic powder

Steps

  1. Carefully place chicken in oil - it will bubble energetically but it will not spit.
  2. cook for 8 minutes (wings for 5 minutes), or until deep golden brown and internal temperature at thickest part is 75°C / 167°F.
  3. Place onto rack and keep warm in oven. Repeat with remaining chicken, coating each batch just prior to cooking.
  4. Serve immediately
